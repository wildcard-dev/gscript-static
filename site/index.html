Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about iinkwell

How is this different from just using Google Docs? +
iinkwell adds professional screenwriting features that Google Docs doesn't have: industry-standard formatting shortcuts, automatic page counting, scene numbering, and dialogue tracking. You get the collaboration benefits of Google Docs plus the specialized tools screenwriters need.
Can I import my existing Final Draft scripts? +
Yes! You can copy and paste your existing scripts into Google Docs, and iinkwell will help you reformat them. We're also working on direct import features for popular formats like .fdx and .fountain.
Do I need to install anything on every computer? +
You only need to install the browser extension once on your main browser. After that, you can access and edit your scripts from any device with Google Docs - even without the extension installed. The extension just adds the advanced features.
Is my work secure and private? +
Absolutely. Your scripts stay in your Google Drive with all of Google's security features. iinkwell only accesses documents when you explicitly activate it, and we never store your content on our servers.
What happens if I cancel my subscription? +
Your scripts remain in your Google Drive forever - they're your documents. You can continue editing them normally in Google Docs. You'll just lose access to iinkwell's premium features like the AI writing assistant and advanced analytics.
