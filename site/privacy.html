Limited Use of Google User Data

G-Script's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. G-Script will transfer your data to Supabase, our database provider, which helps us to process your data and provide the service.

Types of Google User Data Collected

Our application accesses the following Google user data:

Non-sensitive scopes:

  • User email address (https://www.googleapis.com/auth/userinfo.email)
  • User profile information (https://www.googleapis.com/auth/userinfo.profile)
  • OpenID (https://www.googleapis.com/auth/plus.me)
  • Access to Google Drive files (https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.file)

Sensitive scopes:

Access to Google Docs (https://www.googleapis.com/auth/documents) to create, view, and edit Google Documents. We need:

  • Create access to export the user's script as a Google Doc.
  • Read access to check if the user made changes to the Google Doc since it was last updated by our app.
  • Edit access to save changes made in the script editor to the Google Document.

Restricted scopes:

Full access to Google Drive (https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive) to view and edit Google Documents. We need:

  • Read access to import text from an existing Google Document into the script editor.
  • Edit access to save edits made in the script editor to the Google Document.

Purpose of Data Collection and Use

We collect and use Google user data to provide and improve our services, including:

  • Exporting user scripts as Google Documents.
  • Checking for changes made to Google Documents.
  • Saving changes from the script editor to Google Documents.
  • Importing text from existing Google Documents into the script editor.

We will use your data to provide you with the services you requested, such as email notifications and newsletters. We will not sell your data to third parties, but we may share it with our partners who help us provide our services.

Data Storage and Processing

Google user data is stored in Supabase, our database provider, and is processed to facilitate the script-writing process. Data is encrypted in transit to ensure its security.

Data Sharing, Transfer, and Disclosure

We do not share, transfer, or disclose Google user data with any third parties for purposes other than the ones provided in this policy.

Data Protection Mechanisms

Security procedures are in place to protect the confidentiality of your data. We use encryption to protect your information in transit. Our security measures are designed to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

We will retain your personal information for the length of time needed to fulfill the purposes outlined in this privacy policy unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. When the data retention period expires for a given type of data, we will delete or destroy it. You may request for your data to be deleted by contacting us through the provided support channels.
